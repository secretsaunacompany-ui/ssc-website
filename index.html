Our Story

Secret Sauna Co. was founded with the simple goal of enjoying an authentic Finnish riverside sauna. Having spent years exploring hidden local saunas, our aspiration was to recreate those exceptional, nature-immersed moments.

Without access to waterfront property, we envisioned a mobile sauna capable of creating those distinctive riverside experiences anywhere. Following careful study of traditional Finnish sauna design and diligent planning, we succeeded in crafting a sauna that surpassed our own expectations.

Today, our aim is to extend this unique experience to you.

Why Traditional Finnish Saunas?

Not all saunas are created equal. Here's why our construction method delivers a superior experience that lasts decades.

Our Finnish Saunas

Built Like Your Home
  • ✓ Fully insulated envelope (R-13+ walls)
  • ✓ 2x4 framing with vapor barrier
  • ✓ Interior cedar cladding + exterior metal
  • ✓ Proper löyly (steam) at 80-100°C
  • ✓ Heats evenly, retains heat efficiently
  • ✓ Built to last 25+ years outdoors
  • ✓ Proven health benefits over millennia

Barrel Saunas

Common Alternative
  • ✗ No insulation (single-wall construction)
  • ✗ Stave construction with metal bands
  • ✗ Exposed wood inside and out
  • ✗ Difficult to achieve proper heat
  • ✗ Loses heat quickly in cold weather
  • ✗ 5-10 year lifespan typical
  • ✗ Higher energy costs to operate

Infrared "Saunas"

Not a True Sauna
  • ✗ Heats your body, not the air
  • ✗ No steam (löyly) possible
  • ✗ Max temp ~60°C (not hot enough)
  • ✗ No authentic sauna experience
  • ✗ Unproven long-term health claims
  • ✗ Often made with cheap materials
  • ✗ Indoor use only

How We Build

Thoughtful design, honest materials, enduring performance

Design approach

Every sauna begins with questions: How will you use it? Who will gather there? What does the space need to feel right?

We listen first, then design—creating saunas that fit naturally into their surroundings and serve their intended purpose with quiet confidence.

Before production begins, we create detailed 3D renderings of your sauna so you can see exactly what your finished project will look like. This allows us to refine every detail together—from bench placement to window positioning—ensuring the final build matches your vision perfectly.

Materials & construction

We build with Western Red Cedar, locally sourced when possible. Cedar's natural resistance to moisture and decay makes it ideal for sauna construction, while its warmth and aroma create the right atmosphere. For enhanced durability, we offer Thermowood—heat-treated timber that's exceptionally stable and rot-resistant.

Exteriors can be customized to suit your style and setting, from traditional cedar siding to modern metal cladding. Our joinery is simple and strong. Hardware is stainless or galvanized. Insulation is proper. Stoves are reliable. Everything is built to last decades, not years.

Performance & durability

A good sauna heats evenly, retains warmth efficiently, and creates proper löyly—that soft steam that makes Finnish sauna what it is.

We design for performance first: airflow, heat retention, moisture management, and user comfort. Our superior insulation and construction means your sauna requires less fuel to heat and maintain temperature—efficiency that pays for itself over time. Our saunas work reliably in Canadian winters and through years of heavy use.

Working together

From initial consultation through installation, we collaborate closely with our clients. The process is transparent, timelines are realistic, and communication is consistent. We're building something that will last—it's worth taking the time to do it right.

Frequently Asked Questions

What you need to know about working with us

How long does it take to receive a sauna?

Each sauna is unique and timelines can vary; however a typical build takes around 4-6 weeks. You'll be kept informed of your order's progress at every step along the way.

What are your saunas made of?

Generally we build with 2x4 framing, Rockwool insulation, metal exterior cladding, and locally sourced Western Red Cedar interior. We work with a variety of products to ensure each customer is satisfied with their sauna.

What type of foundation is needed?

Most of our residential saunas are built with open deck flooring for natural ventilation and drainage. Ideally the sauna is placed on packed gravel, concrete feet, or pavers—allowing airflow beneath while ensuring water from inside has a place to drain.

What's the difference between wood-fired and electric saunas?

Wood-fired saunas offer authentic heat and atmosphere, ideal for outdoor settings. Electric saunas provide convenient, consistent temperature control—better for indoor or residential installations. We can help determine what's right for your situation.

Do I need a special license to tow a mobile sauna?

No additional permits are required to transport the sauna—it's ready to be taken on your next adventure!

What does the Limited Warranty cover?

The Limited Warranty covers all manufacturing defects including seating, structure, and stove for the first year after purchase (two years for commercial versions). We do not cover trailer or windows. Some wear is expected as wood naturally changes over time.

What kind of maintenance do saunas require?

Each piece of the sauna—from benches to rocks—wears at different rates. Regular cleaning and maintenance is important. Every sauna comes with detailed instructions on weekly, monthly, and yearly care.

How does payment work?

We accept a variety of payments and work with clients to determine the most appropriate plan. Our typical structure is 30% deposit, 30% at halfway, 30% once almost complete, and the final 10% upon delivery.

Do you build infrared saunas?

Generally no. At Secret Sauna Co. we build traditional Finnish saunas which have a proven history over millennia.

What's included in a rental?

Sauna rentals include delivery, setup, firewood, and pickup. We provide everything you need for a complete sauna experience—you just provide the location and the guests.

Do you work outside British Columbia?

While we're based in BC, we've completed projects throughout Western Canada. Contact us to discuss your location and project scope.
